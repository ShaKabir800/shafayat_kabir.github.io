M.S. Thesis

Glass–Silicon Optical Sensor Platform

Development of a glass–silicon bonded platform intended to provide top-side optical access and a silicon DRIE fiber feedthrough for integrated optical sensing.

  • Anodic bonding of borosilicate glass and silicon
  • HF glass thinning and metal-mask process development
  • DRIE-based silicon fiber channel fabrication
